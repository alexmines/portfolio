Company Flyer

The goal of this project was to create a informative flyer that went with the rest of the brand. Being able to take the required information and strategically place it on the two sided flyer.

Type

The fontface used for the flyer are company used type, which is used in other pieces and on their website.

Colour

The colours used in the flyer are all company colours that are found all over their website and other documents.
