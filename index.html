ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Alexandra Mines. I’m currently attending Algonquin College in Graphic Design. Over the past couple of years I've grown an interesting in design, more specifically branding and print. My obsession with the beautiful things around me has made me want to make every design, from print to web, as visually pleasing as can be. As a graphic designer, making each and every piece of design unique, but fitting, is important. There is always room for improvements and changes to make people happy.
