Self Branding

My goal and vision for this Self Branding was to be able to let people decide what they saw in my pieces. Having my hand drawn line art on the back of the business cards allow people to simply make it their own. I wanted people to either colour them in or do some shading or even leave it blank as it is. I want people to be able to see themselves and their potential work in me.

Overall

This piece is important to my portfolio because it demonstrates that I can start from zero and brand someone or something from nothing. This is great for starts up and anyone looking to get branded.

Type

The type used for the business cards was Roboto. The half "A" in the logo is hand drawn and not a specific typeface.

Colour

The colour scheme for the branding is very minimal because I wanted the business cards to be as blank as possible. This allows more room for creativity.
